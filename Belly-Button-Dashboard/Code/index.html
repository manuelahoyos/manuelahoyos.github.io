Demographic Info

In this project, the microbial diversity that inhabit belly buttons is analyzed. For each test subject, the top 10 bacterial cultures found in their navel are displayed in the bar graph. Each bacterial culture is classified by an OTU ID. OTU, or operational taxonomic units, are used to identify groups of bacteria with similar genetic sequences.

In the bubble graph, the top 10 bacterial cultures are also displayed. However, the marker size is determined the amount of that bacterial culture compared to the other bacterial cultures found in that individual's navel.
